Relational Operation
Equals                :  ==  |  x == y |
Not Equal             :  !=  |  x != y |
Less Than             :  <   |  x < y  |
Less Than or Equal    :  <=  |  x <= y |
Greater Than          :  >   |  x > y  |
Greater Than or Equal :  >=  |  x >= y |

Logical Operators
AND : &&     Both Condition needs to be true
OR  : ||     Either Condition needs to be true
Not : !      Inverts the Condition
